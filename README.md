🧠 ***Project Overview***

The **Simhastha Kumbh Mela 2027** presents extreme environmental challenges due to:

- Massive pilgrim influx
- Temporary urban expansion
- Intensive water usage and ritual activities

This project develops a Geospatial + Machine Learning framework to monitor:

💧 Water Quality & Hydrological Stability <br>
🌿 Vegetation Dynamics <br>
🌡️ Land Surface Temperature <br>
🏙️ Land Use / Land Cover Changes <br>
🌫️ Air Quality Trends<br>

 Using multi-temporal satellite data across pre-event, peak-event, and post-event phases.<br>
 
🎯 **Problem Statement**

Large-scale gatherings like Kumbh Mela can:

- Disrupt aquatic ecosystems
- Increase pollution and turbidity
- Cause water scarcity and thermal stress

➡️ A real-time, scalable monitoring system is required for sustainable environmental management.

<h2>🤖Machine Learning</h2>

**Random Forest Prediction Concept**<br>
y_pred = sum(tree.predict(x) for tree in forest) / len(forest)<br>

Why **Random Forest**?<br>

- Handles non-linear environmental patterns<br>
- High accuracy (LULC Accuracy: 98.7%)<br>
- Robust against noise and overfitting<br>

📈 **Key Findings**<br>

🌿 Vegetation<br>
Moderate and stable (NDVI ≈ 0.42)<br>
No significant degradation<br>
💧 Water System<br>
Stable but limited availability (~16.3%)<br>
Vulnerable during peak events<br>
🌡️ Thermal Impact<br>
Built-up areas → higher heat retention<br>
🌫️ Air Quality<br>
PM2.5 → Moderate<br>
NO₂ → Low<br>

🚿 **Bathing Suitability Index** <br>
**Final Score**: 0.291<br>

📌 Based on:

- Water quality<br>
- Crowd pressure<br>
- Pollution indicators<br>

⚠️ **Critical Insights**<br>

🚨 Potential water scarcity during peak Kumbh<br>
🏙️ Ramkund shows high built-up dominance (~40%)<br>
🌊 Kund ecosystems are fragile<br>
👥 Crowd density directly impacts water quality<br>

🛠️ **Recommendations**<br>

⚡ Immediate Actions<br>

- Crowd control during peak days<br>
- Frequent water quality monitoring<br>
- Restricted access zones<br>

🌊 Water Management<br>

- Canal-based replenishment<br>
- Desilting operations<br>
- Groundwater recharge<br>

♻️ Environmental Protection<br>

- Ban single-use plastics<br>
- Install floating waste collectors<br>
- Enforce waste disposal rules<br>

📡 Monitoring System<br>
- Satellite-based alerts (NDVI, NDWI)<br>
- Weekly environmental risk tracking<br>
